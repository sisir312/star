Supporting Lower Class Tribal Communities
Our NGO is dedicated to uplifting lower class tribal populations by addressing their unique challenges. We provide essential resources such as education, healthcare, and skill development programs tailored to their needs. Through advocacy and direct support, we strive to ensure equal opportunities, preserve cultural heritage, and foster sustainable growth within these communities.

Our Mission

We are committed to improving the lives of the tribal community.

Empowering Tribal Youth
Five Star Social Welfare Foundation helped a group of tribal youth complete their education and learn new skills. With our support, they started small businesses and now support their families. Their success inspires others in the community to dream big and work for a brighter future.
